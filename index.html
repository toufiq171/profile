Rethinking AI: Learning the Universe From Zero

A necessity-first thought experiment on machine intelligence and discovery.

Instead of training AI on human-verified datasets, what if we built a neural system as close as possible to human cognition and gave it direct sensory input from its own sensors?

Then we accelerate learning with high compute and strong intrinsic motivation to find patterns in raw experience, without teaching human mathematics or physics from the start.

Could it develop a different way to explain the universe? Could that path help with the inconsistency between general relativity and quantum mechanics?
